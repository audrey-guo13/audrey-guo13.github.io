Who I am

Adventurous enough to try,
detail-obsessed enough to do it well.

I grew up in Shanghai, moved to Silicon Valley at 10, and somehow ended up in a program that has me living in a new city every year — LA, Hong Kong, now Milan.

I'm drawn to business, entrepreneurship, the psychology of why people do what they do, and the occasional perfume rabbit hole.

This website is where I put it all: the projects, the experiments, the thinking out loud. I build things with AI, go deep on whatever I can't stop thinking about, and document all of it publicly — not because I have answers, but because figuring it out in real time is more interesting than pretending I already know.

If something here resonates, I'd genuinely love to hear from you.
